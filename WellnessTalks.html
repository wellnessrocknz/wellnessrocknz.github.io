About Peter Chatteris

Peter Chatteris is a multi-award-winning speaker, author, and coach. Over his 40 years in the public arena, he has inspired millions of people across multiple countries. Peter is renowned for his dynamic presentations that leave a powerful, lasting impression on every audience member, ensuring they walk away with a concrete and actionable plan.

In early 2024, Peter was inducted into The Professional Speakers Association of New Zealand as a full member. He is also a senior member of Toastmasters, having won several public speaking championships in multiple categories.

In 2015, Peter founded The International Speech Academy (INSA), focusing on public speaking and communication excellence. Future plans include setting up a global franchise system for INSA.

Keynote Speech: Unleash the Power

Peter's keynote speech, "Unleash the Power," covers three essential pillars for an enlightened and innovative business: Empowerment, Creativity, and Inspiration.

  • Empowerment: Peter emphasizes the importance of creating an environment where learning continues long after the initial teaching. He introduces exercises to uncover individual motivators, helping people understand themselves better and fostering a more efficient workforce.
  • Creativity: With his background in music and creative writing, Peter brings out the creative potential in everyone. His sessions include lateral thinking exercises and break-out groups to solve seemingly impossible problems, tailored to the specific needs of the industry.
  • Inspiration: Peter's own inspirational story, from being labeled "dumb" and "stupid" to achieving great success, serves as a powerful example. He teaches the audience how to use their own stories to inspire others, creating a workforce led to excellence rather than pushed to mediocrity.
